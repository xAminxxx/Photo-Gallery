Help & Support

How do I track photo likes?

Likes are automatically tracked when users interact with photos. You can view like counts in both the dashboard and individual photo details.

Where does the photo data come from?

Photo metadata is sourced from Pexels API, while engagement data is tracked through our Firebase backend.

How often is data updated?

All engagement data updates in real-time. Photo metadata updates when you refresh the page.

Can I export my analytics data?

Yes! Use the export button in the dashboard to download CSV reports of your analytics data.
